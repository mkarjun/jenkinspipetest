ADVANCED SMART METERING SYSTEM

The Advanced Smart Metering System consists of an Energy meter, Water flow meter, and Gas meter. Smart meter facilitates real-time communication between the customer and the utility providers. By getting real-time information on the usage of utility, this enables consumers to know and also limit the usage of utility. Smart meter analyzes the data received from the sensors to get the unit of utility used and displays the data on a website, which could be viewed on a phone or computer.
